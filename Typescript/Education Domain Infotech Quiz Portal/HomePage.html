Infotech Quiz Portal or better known as IQP is a fully operational web application for people, who are looking for a way to test their knowledge on different programming languages. It is over a decade old and the core members are Bruce Wayne, Bany Allen and Diana Prince. The Admin of this awesome endeavor is Bruce Wayne and the web manager is Clark Kent.
